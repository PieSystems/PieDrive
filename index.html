PieDrive

PieDrive is an upcoming open source project that aims to become your modern day data manager. Our goal is to provide a service that seamlessly integrates your existing cloud storage solutions, like Dropbox, Google Drive, iCloud, Box, etc., and also local storages, like local hard drives, into one and allows you to focus on your data rather than where it is.

On top of this service integration, we want to address cross-cutting concerns like security, privacy, data deduplication, backups, sharing between providers and more.

There are a lot of cloud storage providers out there. Almost all of them offer also a free plan and many of them come for free with a device you buy or a registration you make. This results in your data being split over many different providers and you have to worry about where it is. This comes with the disadvantages of needing to manually migrate data between providers, security and privacy concerns, data duplication, bad usage of the provided free space and the need for automated backup management. All these things and more are what we understand under cross-cutting concerns which PieDrive tries to address and solve. On top of this, we want to try and keep PieDrives core open and free.

Currently, PieDrive is in the pre-prototype stage. This means we are working on getting the first prototype out there and also on setting up all the necessary support processes. If all goes well we expect a user interface prototype around easter 2017 and around June 2017 a first functioning version.
